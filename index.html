On the Enigma of Fear

Become Mia, a paranormal detective searching for her missing father. Investigate and unravel the mysteries of the Enigma of Fear, defeating scary monsters along the way.

A DETECTIVE

Enigma of Fear is an Investigation and Suspense game in Pixel Art/3D where you will be Mia, a young detective investigating the paranormal in a mysterious area called "Perimeter - The place that doesn't exist".

THE CULT OF FEAR

Discover and unravel the mysterious Enigma of Fear: a secret that surrounds the Perimeter with the story of the "Cult of Fear" - a group responsible for the disappearance of countless Ordo Realitas agents.

THE PERIMETER

In the company of her companion, the little dog Lupi, and other friends, Mia is going to investigate the disappearance of her father, Mr. Verissimo, one of the most competent agents in the world and leader of Ordo Realitas, an organization of paranormal detectives.
