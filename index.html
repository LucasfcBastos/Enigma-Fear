ABOUT THE GAME

ENIGMA OF FEAR is a game of exploration, puzzles and survival set in the Order Paranormal RPG universe created by Rafael Lange (Cellbit). You become Mia, a paranormal detective and daughter of the leader of the Ordo Realistas, who is accompanied by her faithful companion Lupi, a fearful dog who helps her on her mission.

ENVIRONMENT

ENIGMA OF FEAR IS AN INVESTIGATION AND SUSPENSE GAME WITH DETAILED HOSTILE ENVIRONMENTS FULL OF MYSTERIES. THE GAME IS MADE IN PIXEL ART AND WITH 3D ELEMENTS, WITH PIXEL ART BEING USED MORE FOR CHARACTERS AND 3D ELEMENTS FOR ITEMS AND SCENERY.

COMBAT

COMBAT IN THIS GAME IS LIMITED BECAUSE IT'S A SURVIVAL GAME, WHERE EVERY ENEMY CAN BE LETHAL. MAKE SURE YOU ALWAYS STAY CLOSE TO YOUR FAITHFUL FRIEND, BECAUSE THE TERRIBLE CREATURE CAN DAMAGE YOUR SANITY AND MAKE YOU PANIC, DON'T LET FEAR MAKE YOU SUCCUMB TO IT.

COMPANIONS

USING YOUR FAITHFUL DOG, FIND CLUES AND HELP MIA GET THROUGH PLACES WITH HER OWN SKILLS. NOT TO MENTION THAT YOU CAN MEET MEMORABLE CHARACTERS WHO CAN HELP YOU ACQUIRE NEW COMBAT AND INVESTIGATION SKILLS DURING YOUR MISSION.

HISTORY OF THE GAME

The paranormal doesn't enter our reality easily. Or at least it should. The world has a solid and fixed base of sanity, so paranormal manifestations of the other side are only found in specific locations or in haunted places. The membrane is what separates our reality from the other side, however, it can be weakened by people's fear, and various occult groups are willing to do whatever it takes to weaken this membrane so that they can summon entities from the other side and gain power.

For this reason, a secret organization called ordo realitas was created. Where paranormal detectives live double lives, investigate and secretly fight against the paranormal, protecting reality from those who just want to plunge the world into chaos. We control mia, an investigator for the order who is looking for her father, mr. Verissimo, who mysteriously disappeared after going to investigate a place called the perimeter, the place that doesn't exist, where a sect called the cult of fear lives.

The cult of fear is responsible for the disappearance of countless agents of the order, and is apparently linked to an entity known as the "god of fear"; it is also known that this group seems to have much bigger plans than any other cult. We suspect that the main members of the sect are the strach family, who lived in a bubble with their own laws, in a kind of bizarre cult of fear. Not that they were already crazy, but they became even more obsessed after a specific event they called the rise of the untrustworthy.
