ABOUT THE GAME

ENIGMA OF FEAR is a game of exploration, puzzles and survival set in the Order Paranormal RPG universe created by Rafael Lange (Cellbit). You become Mia, a paranormal detective and daughter of the leader of the Ordo Realistas, who is accompanied by her faithful companion Lupi, a fearful dog who helps her on her mission.

ENVIRONMENT

ENIGMA OF FEAR IS AN INVESTIGATION AND SUSPENSE GAME WITH DETAILED HOSTILE ENVIRONMENTS FULL OF MYSTERIES. THE GAME IS MADE IN PIXEL ART AND WITH 3D ELEMENTS, WITH PIXEL ART BEING USED MORE FOR CHARACTERS AND 3D ELEMENTS FOR ITEMS AND SCENERY.

COMBAT

COMBAT IN THIS GAME IS LIMITED BECAUSE IT'S A SURVIVAL GAME, WHERE EVERY ENEMY CAN BE LETHAL. MAKE SURE YOU ALWAYS STAY CLOSE TO YOUR FAITHFUL FRIEND, BECAUSE THE TERRIBLE CREATURE CAN DAMAGE YOUR SANITY AND MAKE YOU PANIC, DON'T LET FEAR MAKE YOU SUCCUMB TO IT.

COMPANIONS

USING YOUR FAITHFUL DOG, FIND CLUES AND HELP MIA GET THROUGH PLACES WITH HER OWN SKILLS. NOT TO MENTION THAT YOU CAN MEET MEMORABLE CHARACTERS WHO CAN HELP YOU ACQUIRE NEW COMBAT AND INVESTIGATION SKILLS DURING YOUR MISSION.
